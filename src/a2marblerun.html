The Goal

The goal of the game development pipeline was to make a marble run with at least six distinct sections. Two of those sections must incorporate some non-real world physics. Additionally, one section must lift the marbles with real world physics.

The Theme

I decided to go with a clock theme similar to Mario Kartâ€™s Tick-tock Clock. Each main section has a distinct colour to it with browns and golds connecting each of those sections.

Inverted Gravity Section

When creating the marble run, I prototyped each section in rapid succession to see if my idea has worked. One of the most troubling sections was the inverted gravity section. My initial idea was for them to go all the way up and land on a single clock face. But I found out quickly that the marbles go too fast for the collision system in unity. In the end I made multiple clock faces that they would land on to reduce the air time in which they could accelerate in.
