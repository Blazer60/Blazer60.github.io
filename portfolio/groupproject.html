GDP A2 Group Project

Our first Bournemouth University group project

We were tasked with creating a board game or card game that could be played in under 30 minutes. Our group decided to create a card game inspired by Magic the Gathering, Yu-gi-oh and Hearthstone.

Card Types

There are three card types in our card game. Alchemist, the troops that can be placed on the playing field. Alchemy, spell cards that can be used by Alchemist. And environments, which Alchemists need to be able to cast alchemy by the laws of equivalent exchange.

Elements

Each element has their own style of play. This added more diversity to the game and more choices for the player to think about. Pyro is all about damage. Chipping away at your opponent until they eventually die. Hydro is all about defense. The direct counterpart to Pyro. Electro lets you pile up mana tokens to give you a huge advantage over your opponent and play even more powerful cards. Finally, Geo conserve environment cards so that you can continuously play alchemy as much as you want.
